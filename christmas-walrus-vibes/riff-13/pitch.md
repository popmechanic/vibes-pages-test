# Pitch

Theme: Christmas walrus with a wildcard/experimental twist

**OKLCH Colors for Christmas + Arctic Walrus theme:**
- Deep arctic night sky: oklch(0.15 0.08 260) - dark blue-purple
- Aurora borealis greens: oklch(0.6 0.2 160) - vibrant teal-green
- Aurora pinks/magentas: oklch(0.65 0.25 330) - electric pink
- Warm Christmas red: oklch(0.55 0.22 25) - festive red
- Icy white/blue: oklch(0.9 0.05 220) - frosty white
- Golden ornament: oklch(0.75 0.15 85) - warm gold

**Gradients:**
- Aurora sky: linear-gradient(in oklch, oklch(0.12 0.1 280), oklch(0.2 0.15 200), oklch(0.15 0.12 160))
- Festive accent: linear-gradient(in oklch, oklch(0.5 0.2 25), oklch(0.6 0.18 50))

**Walrus Character Concept:**
Meet "Winslow the Wonderwalrus" - a philosophical walrus who dispenses existential holiday wisdom. He wears a crooked Santa hat and has a magnificent mustache made of icicles.

**Wildcard/Experimental Elements:**
- Glitchy, VHS-style visual effects
- The walrus speaks in cryptic holiday koans
- Users can feed the walrus "cosmic fish" which generate absurdist holiday fortunes
- Retro terminal aesthetic mixed with aurora borealis
- ASCII art walrus that "glitches" occasionally

**App Functionality:**
"Winslow's Cosmic Fish Fortune Machine" - Feed virtual cosmic fish to Winslow and receive bizarre, philosophical holiday fortunes. Track your fortune history and favorite the most profound ones.

**UI Immersion:**
- Scanline/CRT effects
- Glowing aurora animations
- Retro-futuristic terminal feel
- Floating snowflakes or stars
- The walrus ASCII art center stage
