# Pitch

Theme: Christmas-themed educational app with a walrus character

**OKLCH Color Palette:**
- Deep Christmas red: oklch(0.45 0.2 25) - rich, warm red
- Forest green: oklch(0.4 0.15 145) - deep pine green
- Snow white: oklch(0.98 0.01 250) - crisp white
- Gold/Star: oklch(0.75 0.15 85) - warm gold for accents
- Ice blue: oklch(0.85 0.08 220) - cool icy tones for walrus
- Night sky: oklch(0.15 0.05 260) - deep background

**Gradients:**
- Background: Deep night sky with subtle aurora feel
- Cards: Soft snowy gradient

**Walrus Character:**
- Name: "Professor Wally Whiskers" - a wise, jolly walrus scholar
- Personality: Loves teaching, wears reading glasses under his Santa hat, has a warm, encouraging demeanor
- Visual: SVG drawing with Santa hat, tusks, whiskers, and a cozy scarf

**App Concept:**
"Wally's Winter Workshop" - An educational flashcard/quiz app where Professor Wally teaches fun facts about winter, holidays around the world, arctic animals, and more. Users can create their own learning cards and quiz themselves.

**UI Elements:**
- Snowflakes falling (CSS animation)
- Festive border decorations
- Card-based learning interface
- Progress tracking with candy cane progress bars
- Star rewards for correct answers

**Functionality:**
- Create educational flashcards (question/answer)
- Quiz mode to test knowledge
- Track progress and streaks
- Categories for different topics
