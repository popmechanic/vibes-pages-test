**App Concept - "Walrus Wishes":**
A social app where users can share holiday wishes and greetings through their walrus avatar. Each user creates a walrus persona with a unique name, and they can post wishes, react to others' wishes, and spread holiday cheer.

**Features:**
- Create your own walrus persona with a festive name
- Post holiday wishes/messages
- React to others' wishes with festive emojis
- See a live feed of community wishes
- Each walrus has personality traits
