# Pitch

Theme: Christmas Walrus App

**OKLCH Colors for Christmas:**
- Deep Christmas green: oklch(0.35 0.15 145) - rich forest green
- Bright festive red: oklch(0.55 0.2 25) - holly berry red
- Snow white: oklch(0.95 0.01 250) - crisp white
- Gold/warm accent: oklch(0.75 0.15 85) - golden ornament
- Deep night blue: oklch(0.2 0.08 260) - winter night sky
- Icy blue: oklch(0.8 0.08 220) - frost/ice accent

**Gradients:**
- Winter night sky: linear-gradient(in oklch, oklch(0.15 0.05 260), oklch(0.25 0.1 280))
- Aurora effect: linear-gradient(in oklch, oklch(0.3 0.12 160), oklch(0.25 0.1 260))

**Walrus Character Concept:**
- Name: "Wally Whiskersworth" - a distinguished, jolly walrus with a magnificent mustache
- Personality: Warm, wise, loves hot cocoa and giving presents
- Visual: SVG walrus face with Santa hat, big tusks, whiskers

**App Functionality:**
- A personal Christmas wishlist/gift tracker
- Wally helps you track gifts to give and wishes you want
- Holiday countdown feature
- Festive, cozy interface

**Decorative Elements:**
- Snowflakes (CSS animations)
- Twinkling stars
- Candy canes, ornaments as dividers
- Cozy warm glow effects

**UI Feel:**
- Warm and cozy despite the winter theme
- Playful and cheerful
- Personal and intimate gift-tracking experience
