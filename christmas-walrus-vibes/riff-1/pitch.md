# Pitch

For a Christmas-themed walrus app with a minimalist lens:

**Colors (OKLCH):**
- Deep Christmas red: oklch(0.45 0.18 25) - rich, warm red
- Festive green: oklch(0.5 0.15 145) - pine/holly green
- Warm cream/snow: oklch(0.95 0.02 90) - soft white
- Walrus gray-brown: oklch(0.55 0.05 50) - warm gray for walrus
- Gold accent: oklch(0.75 0.15 85) - for stars/decorations
- Dark background: oklch(0.15 0.03 250) - cozy night sky feel

**Gradients:**
- Night sky gradient for background
- Subtle warm glow gradients for cards

**Walrus Character:**
- Name: "Wally Whiskers" - a jolly, gift-giving walrus
- Express through a simple SVG with Santa hat
- Personality: cheerful, loves giving holiday wishes

**App Functionality (Minimalist):**
- A simple holiday wish/greeting card creator
- Users can create wishes that Wally delivers
- Clean, focused interface with just the essentials

**UI Feel:**
- Clean white space, minimal decorations
- Soft shadows, rounded corners
- Focus on the walrus character and wishes
- Snowflake accents but not overwhelming
