# Pitch

Christmas-themed walrus with a "Wildcard" lens - let's go experimental and unconventional!

**OKLCH Colors for a festive but unexpected palette:**
- Deep arctic ocean blue: oklch(0.2 0.08 240) - background
- Icy mint: oklch(0.85 0.15 170) - highlights
- Warm Santa red: oklch(0.55 0.22 25) - festive accents
- Golden tinsel: oklch(0.8 0.18 85) - sparkle elements
- Frosty white: oklch(0.95 0.02 240) - snow/text
- Mysterious purple aurora: oklch(0.5 0.2 300) - unexpected twist

**Gradients:**
- Aurora borealis effect with shifting colors
- Deep ocean to icy surface transitions

**Walrus Character Concept:**
- Name: "Blubbert McTuskington III" - a sophisticated, monocle-wearing walrus philosopher
- Personality: Dispenses absurdist holiday wisdom and existential cheer
- Visual: Large SVG walrus with Santa hat, monocle, and animated whiskers

**Unconventional App Functionality:**
- A "Holiday Wisdom Generator" where Blubbert shares philosophical musings about Christmas
- Users can collect and rate his wisdom nuggets
- Each wisdom has a "profoundness" meter
- Walrus reacts with different expressions based on ratings
- Snowflakes that are actually tiny walrus faces falling

**Immersive Elements:**
- Floating aurora particles
- Animated walrus whisker twitches
- Wisdom cards that wobble like they're floating on arctic water
- Unconventional layout with wisdom bubbles
